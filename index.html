Welcome to Bellevue College's Cooking Competition!

This is where we host our annual cooking competion starting November 1st every year! Our next season will take place in 2023, which will be hosted live on Bulldog News during the month. If you wish to join us in this heated competition, please navigate to our signup page to get started! We can't wait to host you this year!













If you wish to learn more about this event, then keep reading! This annual on-campus cooking competition lasts for 14 days, with most days typically having the following schedule:
  • 8:30-9:00am - Arrival time & station preparation
  • 9:00-10:00am - Work on breakfast dishes
  • 10:00-10:30am - Judgement and scoring of competitors
  • 10:30-11:00am - Break and reseting each station
  • 11:00am-12:00pm - Work on lunch dishes
  • 12:00-12:30pm - Judgement and scoring of competitors
  • 12:30-2:00pm - Break and reseting each station, competitors' meal time
  • 2:00-3:00pm - Work on pastry dishes
  • 3:00-3:30pm - Judgement and scoring of competitors
  • 3:30-5:00pm - Break and reseting each station, faculty feedback to competitors
  • 5:00-6:00pm - Work on dinner dishes
  • 6:00-6:30pm - Judgement and scoring of competitors
  • 6:30-7:00pm - Clean up stations, go home!
During the finale days at the end of the two weeks, the schedule will deviate and be more geared towards our finalists, which for the last day, we will announce the winners after the final dinner service! This will be a very packed, but fun two weeks for everyone competing!






In order to be a competitor in this event, you must have the following requirements:
  • You are a student at Bellevue College
  • You are above the age of 18
  • Your GPA is at least a 3.0 if this is not your first quarter attending
  • You are taking at least 10 credits during the quarter you're competing
  • You have a strong passion for cooking and have the determination to be a winner in this competition!
